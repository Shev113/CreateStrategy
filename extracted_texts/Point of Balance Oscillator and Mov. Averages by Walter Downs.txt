LLV(HIGH,n))/2 + LLV(HIGH,n);Cross(CLOSE,BullFear)Enter Short:n := 12 {Time periods};BearFear := (HHV(LOW,n) - LLV(LOW,n))/2 + LLV(LOW,n);Cross(BearFear,CLOSE)Four-Bar Fear System TestSIGNAL FORMULAS---------------Enter Long:n := 12 {Time periods};BullFear := (HHV(HIGH,n) -
